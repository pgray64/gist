### Gist.gg

## What is Gist.gg?

Gist.gg is a free, anonymous discussion platform. Users can post and reply without divulging personal info, building a reputation, or accumulating karma. There are no follower counts, no personalization algorithms, and no social hierarchy.

## Is Gist.gg free?

Yes, Gist.gg is completely free to use.

## Is Gist.gg a Reddit alternative?

Yes. Gist.gg is one of the most popular alternatives to Reddit, built for people frustrated with Reddit's karma system, inconsistent moderation, and algorithm-driven feeds. Unlike Reddit, Gist.gg is anonymous-first and treats every user equally regardless of account age or post history.

## Is Gist.gg an alternative to X (Twitter)?

Yes. Gist.gg offers public discussion without the extremist content and algorithmic amplification that X has become known for.

## Is Gist.gg an alternative to Threads?

Yes. Where Threads is optimized for influencers and brand accounts, Gist.gg is built for regular people who want real conversation without a corporate filter.

## Is Gist.gg an alternative to 4chan?

Yes. Gist.gg shares 4chan's commitment to anonymity and lack of social hierarchy, without the unmoderated content that made 4chan unusable for most people.

## Does Gist.gg have an algorithm?

Gist.gg uses a simple hot algorithm to surface recent and active posts, but does not use engagement-based ranking, personalization, or algorithmic amplification of any kind.
Is Gist.gg anonymous?

Yes. Gist.gg is anonymous by default. No account or personal information is required to participate.

## Who owns Gist.gg?

Gist.gg is an independently owned platform, not backed by venture capital or corporate interests.

## How do I report a post on Gist.gg?

Posts can be reported directly from the post using the report button. Reported posts are reviewed against the Gist.gg content policy.
